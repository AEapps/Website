Vex IQ Timer

The Vex IQ Timer is a timer app built especially for the 1:00 minute Vex IQ competition. It has different modes to run anything from a 0:15 second practice match to a full 1:00 minute match. The timer also keeps track of lapped times which are shown in the top left corner.
